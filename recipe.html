Chewy Chocolate Caramel Brownies

Ingredients Directions
1 package 2-layer-size German chocolate cake mix 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
3/4 cup butter, melted 2. Grease a 13x9x2-inch baking pan; set aside
1 5 - ounce can (2/3 cup) evaporated milk 3. In large bowl, combine cake mix, melted butter and 1/3 cup of the evaporated milk. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
1 14 - ounce package vanilla caramels, unwrapped 4. Spread half the dough in prepared baking pan; set aside remaining dough. In a heavy large saucepan combine caramels and remaining 1/3 cup evaporated milk.
1 cup chopped walnuts 5. Cook and stir over medium-low heat until melted and smooth. Pour evenly over dough layer in pan.
1 cup semisweet chocolate pieces 6. Sprinkle with nuts and chocolate pieces. Crumble remaining dough evenly over nuts and chocolate pieces.

Strawberry "Cool Brûlée"

Ingredients Directions
2 cups sliced strawberries 1. Divide strawberries among four 8-ounce dessert dishes. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon demarara sugar over strawberries in each dish.
8 teaspoons demarara or turbinado sugar (raw sugar), divided 2. With electric mixer, whip cream to soft peaks. Fold in yogurt and spread evenly over strawberries.
1/2 cup whipping cream 3. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of the remaining demerara sugar over each. Cover and chill for 2 to 6 hours.
1/3 cup lowfat vanilla yogurt

Mocha Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients Directions
1 1/2 cups chocolate milk or milk 1. In a large bowl, combine milk and coffee crystals. Let stand for 5 minutes to dissolve crystals.
2 -3 teaspoons instant coffee crystals 2. Stir in pudding mix and dessert topping mix. Beat with an electric mixer on low speed about 30 seconds or until moistened.
1 4-serving-size package instant devil's food chocolate pudding mix 2. Beat on high speed about 4 minutes or until fluffy.
1 1 1/4 - ounce envelope whipped dessert topping mix 3. Alternately spoon chocolate mousse and strawberries into 6 individual parfait glasses.
2 cups strawberry halves and/or banana chunks 4. Serve immediately or chill until serving time. If you like, garnish with chocolate pieces. Makes 6 servings.
